About

The Brookhaven Neutrino Discovery Initiative (NDI) is an effort here at BNL to get people talking about neutrinos. We bring together experimentalists and theorists to ask and answer the most interesting questions in neutrino physics. "How can we learn more about neutrinos?" and "How can neutrinos help us answer other key questions in physics?" In addition to the scientists at BNL and elsewhere, the NDI hosts a regular journal club and the Brookhaven Neutrino Theory Virtual Seminars.
